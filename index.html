Instructors

Meet the instructors

Lineu Vilanova

Head Instructor

Lineu received his black belt in 2015 after a decade of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu training in Bahia, Brazil. He's been teaching kids and adults since he received his brown belt in late 2012. He's also a registered secondary school teacher in Queensland with a Master's Degree and years of classroom experience.

Lineu is the current Pan Pacific Black Belt Light Weight Masters 1 Gi and No-Gi Champion and Bronze medalist at the 2019 ADCC Gold Coast.

He was also the 2017 Absolute Black Belt No Gi Masters 1 Pan Pacific Champion and Gi/No-Gi 2017 Feather Weight Masters 1 Silver Medalist.

Lineu's approach to Jiu-Jitsu is a no ego, modern, new school game and he has a constant thirst for knowledge and growth.
